More Poems for You ❤️

Just like that a month has passed,

You've been here like the golden hour,

I promise you this love will always last,

Cuz it's true even from afar,


I want you here for all my life,

Now I know choosing you was definitely right,

If not you then your memories would surely suffice,

Because falling for you is like falling from the highest height,


Your voice so good,

Its like a lovely chime,

Your words so beautiful,

They're like a poem's rhyme,


Those late night calls,

And those early morning texts,

Everything just makes me fall,

Harder and harder in your pretext,


Your eyes like an exploding star,

Your voice like the hymn of gods,

Tuning with me like the strings of guitar,

Falling in love with you against all odds,


I just wish to stay with you forever,

Your presence like the river from heaven,

Flowing through Eve's garden nourishing my love like the golden flower,

Baby you and only you can be my eleven.


It's been long now,

Since I left all this behind,

I only left in hopes,

That I would somehow move on with time,


It was neither easy nor was it difficult,

I was probably stuck somewhere in the middle,

Darkness all around, no sign of light,

I thought leaving the only ray was somehow right,


I was stupid if I think about it now,

But I didn't write all those poems just so they could say wow,

I wrote in hopes someone would realise,

How it feels to see this world through my eyes,


I was sitting alone waiting, for a person so pure,

When she came it felt like, for every problem she is the cure,

She entered my life, like autumn after winter,

To have her forever, I'm ready to be a sinner,


With every step she took, flowers bloomed,

In the garden of my life, where everything..hate consumed,

The longer she walked, the quicker I fell for her,

This new feeling in my heart was here to stay, forever,


When she smiles, I fall deeper and,

When she talks, I realise I want only her,

For her I want to be better, for her I want to be the best,

Tell me god, is this true love or just another test?


Her name is srishti, and she is my world,

My home and only place of comfort,

I'll love her forever, in every single life,

I want her forever, everytime like a wife,


So tell me baby, would you love me the same?

Like I do in every mortal plane?

Will you say yes to me in every life?

But first in this, will you be my wife?

When I look at the stars,

And my frown falls apart,

Cuz it's only you who I think about now,

Baby you shine like those blinking stars,

And baby you led me out of this dark path,


You are the reason for my smile,

I have a dream, it's to make you mine,

Your eyes like the cosmic clouds,

And your smile makes me jump and pounce,

I can live for your love cuz it's all I want,

I can let this world burn just for you my girl,
